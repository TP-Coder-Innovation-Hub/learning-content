# Workflow: Learn Anything New with AI as Your Personal Tutor

## What You'll Learn

- How to use AI as an on-demand tutor for any topic
- The Feynman Technique with AI

- Practical exercises for self-directed learning

## Why AI is the Best Tutor You've Never Had

- Available 24/7, patient, never judges
- Adjusts explanation level to match your understanding
- Can generate practice problems, quizzes, and examples
- Explains the same concept 5 different ways until it clicks

## The Feynman Technique with AI

The Feynman Technique: if you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it.

```
Step 1: Ask AI to explain a concept at a beginner level
Step 2: Try to explain it back in your own words
Step 3: Ask AI to identify gaps in your explanation
Step 4: Repeat until your explanation is solid
```

### Exercise: Learn a New Concept

Prompt:
```
Explain [concept] to me as if I'm a complete beginner.
Use analogies from everyday Thai life.
Keep it under 300 words.
Then ask me 3 questions to check my understanding.
```

After answering, continue:
```
Here's my explanation of [concept] in my own words:
[write your explanation]

What am I missing? What did I get wrong?
```

## Creating a Learning Path

Prompt:
```
I want to learn [topic] from scratch.
My background: [what you already know]
My goal: [what you want to be able to do]
Time available: [e.g., 2 hours per week for 4 weeks]

Create a step-by-step learning plan with:
1. Weekly topics in order
2. Key concepts per week
3. One practical exercise per week
4. How to verify I understood correctly
```

## Exercise: Language Learning

Prompt:
```
Teach me 10 essential phrases in [language] for [context, e.g., business meeting / travel / restaurant].

For each phrase:
- The phrase in [language]
- Pronunciation guide
- Literal translation
- When to use it
- Common mistakes to avoid

Then quiz me: give me 5 situations and I'll respond with the right phrase.
```

## Exercise: Technical Concept Breakdown

Prompt:
```
Break down [technical topic] into layers:
1. ELI5 (Explain Like I'm 5)
2. ELI12 (Explain Like I'm 12)
3. ELI-university (Explain at university level)
4. ELI-practitioner (Explain for someone who will use this at work)

For each level, use one analogy and one real-world example.
```

## Spaced Repetition with AI

Prompt:
```
Based on what we covered today about [topic], create 5 flashcards.
For each card:
- Front: A question
- Back: The answer with a short explanation

Format them so I can copy into Anki or any flashcard app.
```

## Free Learning Tools

| Tool | Best For |
|------|----------|
| Claude / ChatGPT (free) | Explanations, practice problems, flashcards |
| Google NotebookLM | Upload textbooks/PDFs, get AI tutor on YOUR materials |
| Perplexity | Research topics with real sources |
| YouTube + AI transcript | Watch a video, paste transcript into AI for summary |
| Anki (free desktop) | Spaced repetition flashcards |

## Key Takeaway

AI is the most patient tutor available. Use it to break down complex topics, practice explanations, and create study materials. The key is active learning — don't just read AI's output, try to reproduce it yourself and ask AI to check your understanding.
